Chronicle of Eternal Vengeance

For eras, Fluffy walked through shadows as an Umbreon without master or purpose, observing the world's decay. His encounter with Aztar wasn't an honorable duel, but a failed execution of his own existence. Aztar pierced his chest with a corrupted electrical energy discharge, seeking his total and definitive extinction.

But the void only filled with a fury and a refusal to die so intense that they pierced the fabric of reality itself, calling the attention of Xyox, the Observer of Collapses. Xyox didn't save Fluffy; Xyox merged with him in a pact of infinite vengeance.

The bell, once a mere decorative ornament, became the Dimensional Containment Seal. The rings, into eyes that see beyond the physical, capable of perceiving intentions and destinies. And Fluffy's soul, into the trigger of a vengeance that transcends time, space, and death.

Since that day, Fluffy no longer hunts for hunger, pleasure, or need. He hunts for a brutal balance: each shot from the Executioner XYR is a correction in reality's fabric, eliminating what Xyox considers "unnecessary noise" and anyone who stands between Fluffy and his final objective: Aztar.
